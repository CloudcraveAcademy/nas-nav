Established in 2018, Nas Navigation Limited is an information technology solutions provider. We provide satellite-based technology solutions designed for Education, Healthcare, Agriculture and Rural Communities Development.

Nas Navigation’s technology provides connectivity which offers the geographic ubiquity that allows users to access quality and equal content.
